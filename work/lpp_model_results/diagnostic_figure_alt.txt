Five rows of paired horizontal grouped-bar charts show observed data followed by four selected model predictions. The left column compares Negative and Neutral recall rates. Emotion-dependent LPP without preferential emotional learning does not reproduce the observed Negative advantage, whereas the three preferential-learning models do. The right column compares Remembered and Forgotten items' mean within-list-centered Early LPP in each category. Preferential emotional learning without LPP shows little separation; General LPP separates both categories to differing degrees; and Emotion-dependent LPP concentrates the separation in Negative items.
